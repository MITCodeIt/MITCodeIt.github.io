In this minilab, you'll familiarize yourself with cloning.

Goals

  • Complete the starter code in order to make the rainbows and lightning bolts fall multiple times.
  • The rainbows and lightning bolts will fall at different times, possibly even randomly.
  • When you play, the hippo should get rainbows - if it touches a lightning bolt, it will lose a heart.

    • What you'll learn

    • Review the use of operators, the pick random block, and conditionals.
    • Use cloning.

    Important things before you begin

    • You only need to edit the Lightning, Heart2, and Heart3 sprites.

    Open the starter project and click Remix.

     When you're finished, be sure to save and share your project, and then add it to the studio here .
